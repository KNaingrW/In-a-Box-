Mexican Burritos

Mexican burritos are a popular dish made from a large flour tortilla filled with various ingredients like meat, beans, cheese, and vegetables. They originated in Northern Mexico and have become a staple in Mexican cuisine. Burritos are known for their large size, making them a filling and satisfying meal. They can be customized to suit individual tastes, making them a versatile and delicious option for any occasion.

Ingredients
  • For the Dough
    1 1/2 cups Makkai ka Atta (maize flour)
    2 garlic cloves, crushed
    1/2 cup Maida
    3 tsp Oil
    1/2 tsp Salt
    As required Water
  • For the Filling
    3 cups Rajma
    3 Onions, finely chopped
    8-9 tbsp Tomato Ketchup
    1 1/2 cups Tomato Puree
    3 tbsp Butter
    3 tbsp Ghee
  • For the Hot Sauce
    1 kg Tomatoes , chopped
    1 Onion, chopped
    2 tsp Oil
    3 tsp Sugar
    1/2 tsp Ajwain
    to taste Salt
  • For Garnishing
    1/2 cup Sour cream
    1 cup Cheese, grated
    1/2 cup Spring onions, chopped
    Jalapenos, sliced

Instructions

  1. Step 1

    Mix all the ingredients together and make a soft dough with some water.

  2. Step 2

    Roll out into thin rounds the size of a chapati and cook on a tawa without any oil.

  3. Step 3

    Keep aside.

  4. Step 4

    Soak the beans overnight, boil, drain and mash them.

  5. Step 5

    Heat ghee and butter together and saute the onions till transparent. Add the beans and mix well. Stir in the puree, ketchup and salt. Cook till the colour darkens, mashing the mixture as it cooks.

  6. Step 6

    Keep aside.

  7. Step 7

    Soak the chillies in half a cup of water.

  8. Step 8

    Saute the onions and add the tomatoes and chillies. Add water if required. Cook till the tomatoes are done, cool and blend it.

  9. Step 9

    Heat the sauce, add ajwain, sugar and salt. Cook for another 10 minutes and keep aside.

  10. Step 10

    In a chapati spread some rajma, hot sauce and sour cream. Sprinkle the onions, jalapenos and cheese.

  11. Step 11

    Wrap it and serve immediately.

Allergic of Mexican Burritos

Mexican burritos may contain allergens such as wheat, dairy, soy, and nuts which can trigger allergic reactions in some people. Symptoms can range from mild to severe, including hives, swelling, difficulty breathing, and stomach pain.

Nutrition of Mexican Burritos

The nutrition of a Mexican burrito can vary depending on its ingredients and serving size. Typically, it contains a balance of macronutrients like carbohydrates, protein, and fat, as well as fiber, vitamins, and minerals from ingredients such as beans, vegetables, and spices. However, some versions may be high in calories, sodium, and saturated fat due to added cheese, sour cream, or fried ingredients. Choosing whole-grain tortillas, lean proteins, and fresh vegetables can make a Mexican burrito a nutritious and satisfying meal option.
